CustomInk.com operates an innovative website that enables people to design and order custom decorated products such as printed t-shirts and embroidered caps for their groups and events including student groups, bands, small businesses, corporations, family reunions, and more. Their t-shirt printing operations accommodate orders of all sizes, ranging from a single t-shirt to quantities in the thousands. In 2005 and 2006, CustomInk was named to Inc. Magazine’s “Inc500” list of America’s fastest-growing private companies. They were also included in Washingtonian Magazine’s last list of “Great Places to Work” in the DC area.

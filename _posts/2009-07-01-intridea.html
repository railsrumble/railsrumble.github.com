Intridea, Inc. develops simple, intuitive web applications and services to help enterprises realize the power of cloud computing and social media. Products include Present.ly, an enterprise microblogging tool, MediaPlug, a media appliance server, and CrowdSound, a social feedback widget.

http://intridea.com applications are based on Ruby on Rails, and the company actively contributes to the Rails community. http://intridea.com is headquartered in Washington, DC. For more information, visit www.intridea.com.

We’re thrilled to announce 200 seats will be available for the Rails Rumble this year!

Through the generosity of our sponsors, Linode and GitHub, we’re able to offer 200 seats for the Rails Rumble this year. Linode is providing 200 Xen powered virtual servers (Linode 360s, to be exact) to deploy your awesome application on. GitHub has matched that donation with 200 private repositories to store your awesome source code in. How awesome is that? (A: pretty awesome!)

Registration will open shortly. Last year, we had 140 teams register for the Rails Rumble. Watch this space or subscribe to our feed and be ready to claim your spot in the Rumble!

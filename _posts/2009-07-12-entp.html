ENTP is a consultancy and web incubator collective based in Portland, Ore. We specialize in implementing big ideas.

We created ENTP with the idea "if you get enough smart people in the room, anything is possible." We are experts at architecting, designing, and implementing web applications using Ruby, Ruby on Rails, and Javascript.

We are also the team behind the popular Lighthouse and Tender Support web applications.

Our services include design, development, consulting and training. For more information please visit entp.com.

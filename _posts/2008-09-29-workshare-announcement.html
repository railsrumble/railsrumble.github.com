This year we are doing something new and fun. It is called the “RailsRumble 2008 WorkShare”. Participating companies will be opening their doors to local teams and offering free work spaces to use during the 48 hour competition.

Do you need a place to set up your rails shop for the Rumble? Looking for a place for your entire team to convene and work together in person? Click here for details on the spaces available, photos and contact information. Don’t wait, spaces are limited, reserve today!

Thanks to all the participating companies and to Leah Silber of Engine Yard for her suggestion and assistance in coordinating the program. If you represent a company that would like to offer a space for the WorkShare please send an email to Leah Silber for more information.

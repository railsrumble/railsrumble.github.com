The Rails Rumble WorkShare is a program in which participating companies open their doors to local teams and offer free work spaces to use during the 48 hour competition. Work spaces for the 2009 event are listed and maintained at the Rumble WorkShare Wiki.

If you’re interested in offering your work space to local teams, please contact Erin Shine or simply add your information to the wiki. Thanks!

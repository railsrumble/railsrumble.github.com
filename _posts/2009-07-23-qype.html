--- layout: post title: Qype ---

Qype is Europe’s largest local search and review site, with over half a million reviews across 9 countries.

We have 2.1 million places across 800 different categories with geo data, photos, and hundreds of thousands of user-generated reviews. All of this data is accessible on our API allowing full integration of Qype content into other applications.

Qype also has applications on Android and iPhone (iTunes) allowing users to find nearby places, read and write reviews, and upload photos.

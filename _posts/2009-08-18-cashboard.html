Cashboard is a Rails-based software application that helps small businesses manage their finances.

Send estimates to potential clients, track hours for your employees, accept online payments for invoices, and track all of your outgoing expenses (coming soon).

Cashboard also offers fully customizable document and email templates – which allows you to maintain your brand identity. Your clients can even login to their own fully branded interface to keep up to date on your project’s progress.
